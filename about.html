Our Story

Built in Bangalore. Built for Bangalore Homes.

Bangalore is changing. New apartments are rising across Koramangala, Indiranagar, Whitefield, and HSR Layout. Families are investing in their first homes, their dream homes. But for too long, they had only two choices — expensive metro-based firms that don't understand local needs, or local contractors who compromise on quality and finish.

NIWASA Interior was founded to change that. We are a premium home interior and modular furniture studio built right here in Bangalore — combining world-class design sensibility with deep local understanding, transparent pricing, and the kind of personal service that only a homegrown company can offer.

We believe that every family in Karnataka — whether in a 2BHK apartment or a sprawling villa — deserves a home that feels intentional, beautiful, and entirely their own. That belief is the foundation of everything we do.
